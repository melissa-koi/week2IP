Would you like to know your Akan name?


Akan names are derived from the Ghanian culture.Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.This is a web application that takes in your birthday and gender, since the male and female names are different,then calculates what your Ghanian(Akan) name would be.
